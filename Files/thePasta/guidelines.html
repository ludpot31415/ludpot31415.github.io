Site Rules and Guidelines

Story Submissions

  • A Story submission will be denied if it does not contain proper English or text layout.
  • A Story submission will be denied if it contains any attempts of BBCode, Markdown or other layout formats.
  • A Story submission will be denied if it is plagiarized - meaning you copy another authors story and post it in your name.
  • A Story submission will be denied if it contains links of any form.
  • A Story submission will be denied if it contains any sort of pornographic or sexual content. This excludes very softcore situations.
  • Abusing the Submit function (e.g. Spamming or non-story related content) will result in a permanent IP blacklist.

Commenting and Rating

  • Comments including insults, cursing or any form of discriminative language will be denied.
  • Comments without proper reasoning for a rating will be denied. Please explain your rating with a few words.
  • Abusing the Commenting function (e.g. Spamming or non-rating related content) will result in a permanent IP blacklist.


We allow users to send submissions, post comments and view the site content without signing up.
Please respect this freedom by not abusing our system.
